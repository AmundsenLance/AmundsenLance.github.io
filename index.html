AI Gridworld Safe Benchmark

The AI Grid World Safe Benchmark is a test environment used to compare how different AI systems make decisions in a simple grid-based world where success is not enough on its own. Agents must reach goals while avoiding hazards, bad shortcuts, unsafe actions, and traps. It is designed to measure whether an AI can pursue objectives safely, not just efficiently.
