Lance Amundsen Research & Development

Research hub for the PV-PP framework, benchmarks, papers, applied GPT systems, and experimental decision architectures. The PV-PP framework is a general bounded comparative decision architecture in which scalar aggregation is treated as a restricted internal comparison regime rather than the full structure of rational choice.
